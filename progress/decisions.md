# Decision Log

Every meaningful decision the STUDENT makes — project choices, tech choices,
design/schema choices, scope cuts — recorded so future sessions (and future
the-student) know what was decided and WHY. Claude appends an entry whenever a
decision is made; the rationale should be in the student's own words where possible.

Format:
## YYYY-MM-DD — <decision>
- **Context:** what question we were facing
- **Options considered:** A vs B (one line each)
- **Decision & rationale (student's words):** ...
- **Revisit if:** condition under which this should be reconsidered
